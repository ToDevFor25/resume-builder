Privacy Policy

Last updated April 2025  ·  Effective immediately

StretchRole ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of healthcare professionals who use our career positioning platform. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

We collect only what's necessary to provide and improve the service:

  • Account information — your name, email, and profile image from the OAuth provider you choose (LinkedIn, Google, or Apple). We never see or store your social login password.
  • Career profile data — your current level, target level, and focus area that you set during onboarding or in your Profile.
  • Resume content — the text of any resume you upload as your master resume or for a specific session. This is used solely to power your tool results.
  • Usage data — which tools you use and when, so we can enforce plan limits and improve the product. We do not sell this data.
  • Feedback — thumbs up/down ratings you submit on tool results.

How we use your data

  • To deliver personalized results from our AI tools (resume optimization, interview prep, job analysis, reference letters)
  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • To enforce plan limits and manage billing (via Stripe)
  • To improve our models and product over time using aggregated, anonymized signals
  • To send transactional emails (e.g. receipt of a purchase). We do not send marketing email without explicit opt-in.

What we never do

  • We never sell your personal data or resume content to employers, recruiters, or any third party
  • We never share your identity with any employer or job board without your explicit action
  • We never use your resume content to train AI models without anonymization and aggregation
  • We never store your OAuth passwords — authentication is handled entirely by Clerk

Third-party services

We use a small set of trusted third-party services to operate StretchRole:

  • Clerk — authentication and identity management. Subject to Clerk's privacy policy.
  • Anthropic — AI model provider. Prompts and resume content are sent to Anthropic's API to generate results. Anthropic's data retention policies apply.
  • Stripe — payment processing. We never store full card numbers. Subject to Stripe's privacy policy.
  • Vercel — hosting and serverless infrastructure. Anonymized request logs may be retained per Vercel's policy.

Data retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your master resume or your entire account at any time from your Profile page. Upon account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Cookies and local storage

We use browser localStorage to store your career profile preferences and usage counts on your device. We use session cookies set by Clerk for authentication. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

StretchRole is intended for working professionals. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be communicated via email or an in-app notice at least 14 days before taking effect. Continued use of the platform after that date constitutes acceptance.
