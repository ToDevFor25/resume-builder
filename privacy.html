Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026  ·  Ascend RX LLC  ·  Questions? [[email protected]]

Our privacy commitment in plain English: We collect only what we need to run the platform. We never sell your data. We never use your resume to train AI models. Your career history is yours — we're just here to help you present it better.

01 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how [Ascend RX LLC] ("Ascend RX," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our website at ascendrx.com and our career platform services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. This policy applies to all users of Ascend RX, including free and paid subscribers.

02 What We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

03 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide the Service — processing your resume, generating optimized content, and delivering tool outputs
  • To manage your account — authentication, account settings, and subscription management
  • To process payments — billing, invoicing, and subscription management via Stripe
  • To improve the Service — understanding how features are used to make them better (using aggregated, anonymized data only)
  • To communicate with you — transactional emails (receipts, account updates), and with your consent, product news and updates
  • To ensure security — detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations — responding to lawful requests from government authorities

04 What We Don't Do

We make these commitments clearly and without qualification:
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties — ever
  • We do not use your resume or career content to train AI models without your explicit, opt-in consent
  • We do not share your resume or career content with other users of the platform
  • We do not share your career profile data (role level, target level, focus area) with employers, recruiters, or staffing agencies
  • We do not transmit, record, or store voice audio — all speech processing happens locally in your browser
  • We do not share your information with employers, recruiters, or staffing agencies
  • We do not use your data for behavioral advertising or sell it to data brokers
  • We do not store your full payment card information — Stripe handles all payment data

05 Third-Party Services

We use a limited set of trusted third-party services to operate the platform. Each has its own privacy practices:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — powers the AI processing of your resume and career content. Content sent to the API is subject to Anthropic's API data usage policy. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train models by default.
  • Stripe — handles all payment processing. Your payment information is stored and managed by Stripe under their privacy policy and PCI DSS compliance.
  • Clerk — manages user authentication via Google, LinkedIn, Microsoft, and Apple sign-in. Clerk stores your authentication data securely.
  • Vercel — hosts the Service infrastructure. Vercel may log standard request data for security and performance purposes.
  • Analytics provider — we use a privacy-first analytics tool (such as Plausible or Fathom) that does not use cookies, does not track individuals across sites, and does not sell data.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or other advertising-focused tracking tools.

06 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. Specifically:

  • Account data — retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Career profile data — current level, target level, and focus area are stored in your user profile and retained while your account is active. You may update or delete this data at any time from your account settings.
  • Resume and career content — retained as long as you have an active account and have saved content in your history. You can delete individual saves at any time.
  • Payment records — retained for 7 years as required by applicable tax and financial regulations.
  • Usage logs — retained for up to 12 months for security and performance purposes, then deleted or anonymized.

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

07 Security

We take data security seriously and implement industry-standard measures to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Secure, access-controlled infrastructure through Vercel
  • Authentication managed through Clerk with industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocols
  • Payment data handled exclusively by Stripe's PCI DSS-compliant systems
  • Limited employee access to user data on a need-to-know basis

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at [[email protected]].

08 Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the following rights with respect to your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to our processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Withdrawal of consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [[email protected]]. We will respond to all requests within 30 days.

09 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know — the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect about you
  • Right to delete — request deletion of personal information we have collected
  • Right to opt-out — opt out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights

To submit a CCPA request, contact us at [[email protected]] with the subject line "CCPA Request."

10 Cookies and Tracking

We use a minimal set of cookies necessary to operate the Service:

  • Session cookies — maintain your login state while you use the platform. These are deleted when you close your browser.
  • Preference cookies — remember your settings and preferences across sessions.

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, or cross-site tracking. Our analytics tool (Plausible or Fathom) is cookieless and privacy-first.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling session cookies will prevent you from staying logged in.
