FAQ

Questions we actually get asked.

Will the AI make up experience I don't have? ▾
Never. This is our core commitment. Ascend RX will never invent companies, job titles, dates, degrees, certifications, or metrics that aren't in your original resume. It rewrites and elevates what you've already done — it doesn't fabricate anything. You can verify every word.
Is this only for healthcare operations? ▾
Yes — intentionally. Ascend RX is built specifically for healthcare operations professionals pursuing Director-to-VP transitions. The AI understands the language, competencies, and hiring patterns of this specific career track. Generic career tools don't know what a VP of Clinical Operations actually does. We do.
How is this different from ChatGPT or generic AI tools? ▾
ChatGPT will write you a resume — but it doesn't know the difference between a strong VP of Operations resume and a generic one. Ascend RX uses a purpose-built four-stage pipeline: job analysis, strategic planning, content rewriting, and validation scoring. Every output is designed around what healthcare ops hiring managers actually look for.
What file formats does the resume tool support? ▾
You can upload your resume as a PDF, DOCX, TXT, or RTF file. The output is always a professionally formatted DOCX file ready to send — with your choice of Executive, Modern two-column, or ATS-clean single-column layout.
When will the other tools (Interview Coach, Salary Simulator, etc.) launch? ▾
We're building and launching modules sequentially so each one is excellent before we move to the next. Pro subscribers get early access to every tool as it goes live. Sign up now to lock in founding-member pricing before rates increase.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▾
Yes — cancel anytime with no contracts, no penalties, and no questions asked. Active Search subscribers keep access until the end of their billing month. Starter annual subscribers keep access until the end of their annual period. Most users on Active Search stay 2–3 months then cancel when they land their role — that's exactly what the plan is designed for.
Is my resume data private and secure? ▾
Your resume data is used only to power your optimization. We don't sell it, share it, or use it to train models. Your career history stays yours.
What if I'm not satisfied with the output? ▾
Run it again with different settings — try a different rewrite intensity, positioning level, or template. Each optimization is a new pass. Pro users get unlimited runs, so there's no risk in experimenting until you get exactly what you want.
